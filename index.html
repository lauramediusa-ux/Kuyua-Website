How does
it work?

Step One
📍

Map Your Location

Upload your asset locations and instantly see them plotted on our global risk intelligence map. Connect addresses, GPS coordinates, or portfolio data in seconds — no GIS expertise required.

Step Two
📊

Analyze Environmental Risks

Get a comprehensive risk assessment for each asset, covering physical climate hazards (flood, drought, heat, wildfire), nature dependencies, and regulatory exposure — powered by IPCC and TNFD science.

Step Three
💰

Translate Risks Into Financial Impact

Convert environmental scores into monetary terms. Understand Value at Risk (VaR), stranded asset exposure, and potential write-downs — aligned with TCFD, TNFD, and CSRD reporting requirements.

Step Four
🔍

Risk Evolution Foresight and Monitoring

Track how risks evolve across climate scenarios (1.5°C, 2°C, 4°C). Receive alerts when new hazards emerge and monitor your portfolio's risk trajectory in real time with automated updates.

Step Five
📋

Act and Report With Confidence

Generate audit-ready reports aligned with CSRD, TCFD, TNFD, and EU Taxonomy. Export boardroom-ready decks and regulatory filings with a single click — and turn risk insight into strategic action.
