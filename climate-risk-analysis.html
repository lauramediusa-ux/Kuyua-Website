The Essentials

What is climate risk analysis?

Climate risk analysis helps you understand how climate-related events — such as extreme heat, drought, or heavy rainfall — can affect your business. Kuyua analyzes these risks at a location level, providing clear insights into where your operations and supply chain are exposed, and how these risks may evolve over time.

Why it matters

Why climate risk matters
for your business

Climate risk is no longer an abstract concept. It shapes how companies operate, invest, and report today.

Operational disruption

Extreme weather can impact production, logistics, and supply chain continuity.

Financial exposure

Climate risks can lead to increased costs, asset damage, or reduced productivity.

Regulatory pressure

Frameworks like CSRD and TCFD require companies to assess and disclose climate risks.

Long-term resilience

Understanding risks early helps you plan and adapt for the future.
